What I Worked On (Sample Document for RAG Testing)

This is a sample document used to test Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)
in NC. In a real application, you would replace this with your own documents,
knowledge base articles, or documentation.

The key insight behind RAG is simple: instead of asking an AI model to answer
from memory alone, you first retrieve relevant passages from your own data,
then include those passages as context in your prompt. This grounds the AI's
response in your actual content rather than its training data.

RAG works well for:
- Internal knowledge bases and documentation
- Customer support FAQ systems
- Legal document analysis
- Research paper summarization
- Product catalog search

To build a RAG system in NC, you use the gather statement to load documents,
then pass relevant excerpts to the AI via the ask AI to instruction.

This approach gives you accurate, grounded answers without fine-tuning a model.
